Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 9, 2026

Overview

SudokuAI ("the App") is a free puzzle game developed by Kaan. Your privacy is important to us, and this policy explains how we handle your information.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • No personal information (name, email, phone) is collected
  • No location data is accessed
  • No usage analytics or tracking tools are used
  • No cookies or similar technologies are employed
  • No data is shared with third parties

Local Storage

The App may store game progress and preferences locally on your device using standard browser/app storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages and does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
