FAQ

How do I play?

Select a difficulty level to start a new game. Tap an empty cell, then tap a number from the numpad to place it. Wrong numbers will be highlighted in red.

How does the Hint feature work?

Tap the Hint button to get a logical explanation for the next step. The hint panel shows which technique to use (e.g., Naked Single, Hidden Single) and highlights the target cell.

Can I undo a move?

Yes! Tap the Undo button to revert your last move. You can undo multiple moves in a row.

What are Notes?

Toggle Notes mode to add pencil marks (candidate numbers) to cells. This helps you keep track of possible values without committing to a number.

Is my data collected?

No. SudokuAI does not collect any personal data. See our Privacy Policy for details.
